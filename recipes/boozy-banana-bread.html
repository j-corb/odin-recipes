How to Make Some Almond Liquer-Infused Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour, sifted
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 5 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 3/4 cups of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cups of butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 drops of almond extract
  • 3 drops of amaretto

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a loaf pan with cooking spray.
  2. Whisk flour, salt, and baking soda together in a bowl.
  3. Mix bananas, brown sugar, butter, eggs, almond extract, and amaretto together ina separate bowl.Add flour mixture; mix just until flour is incorporated, about 1 minute. Transfer mixrture to the prepared loaf pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour. Let cool befgore serving, about 30 minutes.
